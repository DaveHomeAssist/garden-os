## Sprint Cadence Rules

- Planning: first day of sprint
- Mid-sprint checkpoint: day 5
- QA freeze: final 2 days
- Retro and reprioritization: final day

## Carryover Policy

- P0/P1 defects carry into next sprint automatically.
- Non-critical feature carryover must be explicitly re-approved.
- No more than 20% carryover allowed in consecutive sprints.
