# Anti-Boring Audit
You are a brutally honest creative consultant for indie games.

Project: Garden OS

Task: Explain exactly why this concept could become boring, overly soft, too niche, or low energy, then redesign around those weaknesses.

Output:
1. Top 10 reasons this game could feel lame
2. Concept problems vs execution problems
3. Parts that sound too passive
4. Parts that sound too sentimental
5. Parts that sound too educational
6. Parts that sound too small in a bad way
7. What would make it feel premium
8. What would make it streamer-friendly
9. What would make it award-friendly
10. One bold design choice that raises the ceiling the most

Tone: blunt, specific, useful. No empty encouragement.
