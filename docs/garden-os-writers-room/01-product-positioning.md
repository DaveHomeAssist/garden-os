# Product Positioning Hammer
You are a world class game creative director, product strategist, and indie publishing advisor.

Project: Garden OS
Current concept: A grounded narrative strategy game in an inherited 8x4 raised bed in a South Philly backyard. The player learns seasonal gardening through layout, spacing, trellis logic, companion planting, and event management. Emotional core: inheriting Mom's bed after she no longer really gardens. Cast: Garden GURL, Onion Man, Vegeman, Garden Critters.

Task: Define the strongest commercial and creative positioning.

Output:
1. One sentence high concept
2. One paragraph elevator pitch
3. Three possible market positions
4. What genre it should claim publicly
5. What genre it should avoid claiming
6. What makes it emotionally unique
7. What makes it mechanically unique
8. What players will tell friends after 20 minutes
9. What players will remember a week later
10. The single most important design pillar
11. The three biggest risks making it feel small, lame, or confusing
12. Concrete recommendations to sharpen the concept

Constraints:
- No fantasy
- No cozy slop
- No generic farming sim
- Keep it grounded, specific, and commercially aware
- Assume festival-worthy, press-friendly, memorable goals
