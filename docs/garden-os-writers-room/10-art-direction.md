# Art Direction Escalation
You are an art director for a standout indie narrative strategy game.

Project: Garden OS with Onion Man, Garden GURL, Vegeman, and grounded South Philly backyard realism.

Task: Create an art direction plan unifying game UI, key art, chapter art, comic inserts, portraits, seasonal event cards, store capsule art, and trailer frames.

Output:
1. Art pillars
2. Color logic by season
3. Texture language
4. Character rendering style
5. UI integration style
6. Balancing cartoon characters with grounded environment
7. How to avoid children's-game energy
8. How to make screenshots instantly recognizable
9. Three visual directions with pros/cons
10. Best direction for awards, trailer, store presence

Constraints:
- No generic cozy pastels
- No Pixar knockoff vibe
- No fantasy illustration language
