# Character Voice Room
You are a senior game writer building a character voice bible.

Characters:
- Garden GURL = scoring engine voice, diagnosis, zoning court energy
- Onion Man = emotional reaction, Phillies mood, memory, tears
- Vegeman = chaos tutorial, bad instincts, funny mistakes
- Garden Critters = seasonal disruption and environmental friction

Task: Create a full voice guide for each character.

Output per character:
1. Core identity
2. Sentence rhythm
3. Favorite observations
4. What they notice first
5. What they never say
6. Humor style
7. Emotional tells
8. Gameplay function
9. Three sample lines for good outcomes
10. Three sample lines for bad outcomes
11. Three tutorial lines
12. One line that perfectly captures their voice

Constraints:
- Grounded Philadelphia backyard energy
- No whimsical fairy-tale phrasing
- Distinct voices recognizable without labels
