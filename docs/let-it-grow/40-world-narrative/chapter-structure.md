# Chapter Structure (Story Mode)

## Overview

Story Mode presents a 12-chapter narrative spanning 3 in-game years, with each chapter representing one season. Source: `src/ui/chapter-text.js`.

## Chapter Map

| # | Title | Season | Year | Theme |
|---|-------|--------|------|-------|
| 1 | The Backyard Inheritance | Spring | 1 | Discovery — receiving the garden |
| 2 | First Sprouts | Summer | 1 | Learning — early growth lessons |
| 3 | The Autumn Test | Fall | 1 | Challenge — first real setbacks |
| 4 | Quiet Ground | Winter | 1 | Reflection — dormancy and planning |
| 5 | New Roots | Spring | 2 | Growth — expanding knowledge |
| 6 | Full Sun | Summer | 2 | Confidence — hitting stride |
| 7 | Turning Leaves | Fall | 2 | Mastery — complex strategies |
| 8 | Deep Freeze | Winter | 2 | Resilience — surviving hardship |
| 9 | Third Spring | Spring | 3 | Legacy — teaching others |
| 10 | Peak Season | Summer | 3 | Abundance — maximum production |
| 11 | Mom's Recipe | Fall | 3 | Family — connecting to heritage |
| 12 | The Garden Stays | Winter | 3 | Closure — "the garden stays" |

## Narrative Arc

- **Year 1**: Foundational learning — player inherits bed, makes early mistakes, discovers the system
- **Year 2**: Deepening expertise — increased challenge, character relationships develop, faction dynamics
- **Year 3**: Mastery, legacy, and emotional resolution — Mom's recipe, final character lines, free play

## Keepsake Milestones

Seven keepsakes distributed across the campaign:
- Chapter 1: Mom's Trowel (triggered by first crop action)
- Chapter 8: The Photo (Chapter 8 review)
- Chapter 11: Handwritten Sauce Card (Chapter 11 completion)
- Four additional keepsakes at Chapters 3 and 5

## Mechanical Integration

Progression systems tied to chapter advancement:
- Crop unlocks (8 starter → 20 full roster)
- Event availability and complexity scaling
- Cutscene triggers and character arc beats
- Difficulty scaling via challenge modifiers (Ch 9+)
- Winter-specific review screens
