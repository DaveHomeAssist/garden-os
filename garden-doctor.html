Garden Doctor keeps symptom triage inside Garden OS.

Select the crop, season, and visible symptoms. Garden Doctor ranks the most likely causes, shows why they fit, and points you to the next checklist instead of sending you back to search-engine roulette.

The logic is static and local-first. No API calls, no backend, and no black box: every diagnosis is just symptom rules cross-checked against the same crop families Garden OS already uses for planning.
