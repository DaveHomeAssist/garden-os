🌼 What It Does

Everything you need for a thriving garden

Garden OS handles the tricky parts of planning so you can focus on the fun part — growing things.

🗺️
Plan your garden layout
Drag and drop vegetables into your raised bed grid. See your whole plan at a glance before you plant a single seed.
📅
Know what to plant when
Get planting windows based on your hardiness zone. No more guessing whether it's too early or too late in the season.
🤝
Find good plant neighbors
Some plants love growing near each other. Others don't get along at all. Garden OS tells you which is which — in plain English.
🔨
Build your raised bed
Follow a step-by-step guide to build or set up your raised bed, including what you'll need for cages, stakes, and supports.
🌻 How It Works

From blank bed to full plan in four steps

You don't need to know anything about gardening software. Just follow along.

1
Open the Planner in your browser
Click "Open Planner" and it loads right away — no downloading, no sign-up, no passwords. It works in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.
2
Tell it about your garden
Enter your raised bed size, where you live (for frost dates), and how much sun your garden gets. Takes about two minutes.
3
Add your vegetables
Pick from a list of common vegetables and place them in your bed. The planner gives you a score and explains what's working well and what to adjust.
4
Save and print your plan
Save your garden plan as a file you can open later, or print it out to keep by your raised bed all season. Your plan is yours — it never lives on anyone's server.

💡 Good to Know

A few things that make Garden OS different

It was built to be simple, private, and long-lasting.

🔒
Your data stays on your computer
Nothing you enter ever gets sent to a server. Your garden plan is saved locally on your device and belongs to you completely.
📡
Works without internet
Once you've opened it, Garden OS keeps working even if your Wi-Fi goes out. Perfect for using outside at the garden.
💸
Completely free
No subscription, no trial period, no "premium" features. Everything is free, forever. It's open source software.
💾
Save and come back anytime
Your plan saves automatically while you work. You can also save it as a file to your computer and open it again next season.
